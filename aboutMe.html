Hi there! My name is Edy and welcome to my learning journal.

After several months of starting learning how to code, I've finally decided to start my journey to become a Full Stack developer. Currently I'm focusing in learning all about the FrontEnd area, expecting to start learning BackEnd right after.

How I stay committed to learning

My biggest advice to someone who's trying to learn how to code and be commited to it is to find a "coding partner". You'll UNDOUBTLY be more motivated to code and to learn if you feel that you are in a sort of "competition" with a friend of yours.

Also, share your work with people that you find important, it'll boost your motivation and you'll find yourself eager to show them your new project.

How I get started

I started learning code back in 2022 during the end of the year, learning the basics in Python and then learning more back at school, where I started my career as a programming student.
However, I started learning Web-Dev in 2023, when a certain opportunity to work showed to me, which, unfortunatly, couldn't come to a reality but at the end of the day, it started that desire inside me to develop in the Full Stack area with a hopeful desire to turn this into my source of income and my business in the future.
